Lasagna

This is my mom special homemade lasagna recipe whit a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filling

I have found none better anywhere. Serve whit a leafy green salad and crusty garlic bread

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagne
  5. Bake the lasagne
