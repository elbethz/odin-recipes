Better Baked Beans

Tangy, sweet, old-fashioned baked beans are made the easy way using canned beans. This is my grandma's favorite semi-homemade recipe.

She makes it every Thanksgiving, and we usually end up scraping the pan clean! There are never any leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of baked beans
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 teaspoons prepared yellow mustard
  • 3 slices of bacon

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients and preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. stir together baked beans, onions, syrup, brown sugar, ketchup and mustard in a large bowl
  3. Pour into a 9x13-inch baking dish. Lay bacon slices across the top
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until the bacon is browned and the beans have thickened, 35 to 40 minutes
